THE ROOF TERRACE

The south facing roof terrace has views on the village, the countryside and the sea. On clear days you can also see Marocco and The Strait of Gibraltar. Choose your favorite corner to enjoy an aperitif and a bite from the ‘honesty bar’, loose yourself in a book or get energized by the sun.

BREAKFAST

Within a radius of 300 meters you will find typical bars and patisseries that serve a delicious breakfast at democratic prices (from €1,70). You can also choose to prepare your breakfast in our fully equipped kitchen. In our patio you’ll find a coffee and tea corner.
