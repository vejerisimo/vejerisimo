A COSY ATHMOSPHERE

WITH PERSONAL ATTENTION

VEJERÍSIMO is a Guest House situated in a quiet little street in the heart of the historic center of Vejer de la Frontera. The restaurants, typical bars and places of interest are just a footstep away. Next to the house you will find the old city wall with “La Puerta Cerrada” (Closed Door), one of the 4 old entrances to the village.

The centuries-old house has been restored maintaining Moorish features but with a 21th century twist. The house has 4 double rooms situated around a typical patio. It has a lot of cozy corners: read a book next to the patio’s library or enjoy the roof terrace with its long chairs and chill-out corners. We promise you an unforgettable stay!
